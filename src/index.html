FlakeSecure

Authentication reimagined. Log into any website using your FlakeSecure app and browser extension — no passwords stored anywhere, no data at risk, no compromise.

🚧

FlakeSecure is under active development

We're building something that puts security first and friction last. Join the waitlist to be among the first to try it — no password required to sign up, naturally.

How it works

Three steps to secure login.

01

Browser Extension Detects Login

When you visit a FlakeSecure-enabled site, the extension automatically recognises the login page and initiates an authentication request.

02

Approve on Your Phone

A push notification is sent to your FlakeSecure app. One tap — and your biometrics or PIN confirm it's really you. No code to type.

03

You're In — Instantly

The extension completes the handshake and you're logged in. Cryptographically verified. No password ever transmitted.

Security Architecture

Security isn't a feature.
It's the foundation.

FlakeSecure was designed from the ground up with a zero-knowledge, zero-trust philosophy. Here's what that means in practice.

🔑
End-to-End Encryption
Every authentication token is encrypted on-device using asymmetric key pairs. SnowyStudio servers never see your private key — only a public signature.
👁️
Zero-Knowledge Architecture
We cannot see your credentials. Zero-knowledge proofs allow us to verify your identity without ever knowing what that identity contains.
📵
No Password Storage
FlakeSecure doesn't store passwords — not on our servers, not in your browser. There's nothing to breach, nothing to leak.
📱
Device-Bound Keys
Private keys are generated and stored exclusively on your device using the secure enclave. They never leave your hardware — ever.
🛡
Phishing-Resistant
Because there's no password to steal, phishing attacks are fundamentally ineffective. Approval requests are cryptographically bound to the requesting domain.
🔄
Token Rotation
Session tokens expire and rotate automatically. Stolen tokens become worthless within seconds. Every session is cryptographically fresh.
🌐
Open Protocol
The FlakeSecure authentication protocol will be publicly auditable. Security through obscurity is not security — we believe in transparency.
✅
Multi-Factor by Default
"Something you have" (your phone) plus "something you are" (biometrics) is built-in by default — no optional toggle required.

Platform
