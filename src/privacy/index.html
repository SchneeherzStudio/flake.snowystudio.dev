Privacy Policy

How SnowyStudio collects, uses, and protects your data.

Last updated: January 2026

1. Controller

The data controller responsible for processing your personal data is SnowyStudio (see Imprint for full contact details). If you have any questions regarding data protection, please contact us at legal@snowystudio.dev.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 FlakeTrader (Discord Bot)

  • Your Discord user ID (to associate your trades and portfolio)
  • Server (guild) ID of the Discord servers where the bot is active
  • Trade history, portfolio balances, and leaderboard entries
  • Timestamps of commands issued

We do not collect your email address, real name, or any financial information via FlakeTrader.

2.2 FlakeSecure (Coming Soon)

  • Public cryptographic keys generated on your device
  • Authentication event metadata (timestamps, requesting domain)
  • Optionally: your email address for account recovery purposes

FlakeSecure is built on a zero-knowledge principle. Private keys never leave your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

2.3 Website

  • Server access logs (IP address, browser type, referrer) for security and analytics
  • Email addresses submitted via waitlist forms (FlakeSecure early access)

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): Processing necessary to provide the services you use.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): Server logs and security monitoring.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): Waitlist registrations and optional communications.

4. Data Retention

FlakeTrader data is retained as long as your account is active or as required to provide the service. You can request deletion at any time. Access logs are purged after 30 days. Waitlist emails are deleted upon request or once the waitlist period ends.

5. Third Parties

We do not sell your data to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Infrastructure providers (hosting, database) under GDPR-compliant data processing agreements
  • Discord (for bot functionality — governed by Discord's own Privacy Policy)
  • Market data providers (no personal data is shared)

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to: access your data, request rectification, request erasure ("right to be forgotten"), data portability, restriction of processing, and to object to processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at legal@snowystudio.dev.

7. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. We use only essential session data required for functionality.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including TLS encryption in transit, access controls, and regular security reviews.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be communicated via our website or email (where applicable). Continued use of our services after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related queries: legal@snowystudio.dev
